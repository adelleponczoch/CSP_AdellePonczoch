Why this order?

First of all, nothing can top Sea Salt Carameled Chocolate, that's just a given. They are sweet and salty, and melt in your mouth perfectly. Reeses are second because they are always delicious, and are a great on the go snack. The peanut butter and chocolate blend seamlessly together and are utterly delicious. Butterfingers are third because of their perfect crunch and surprisingly good aftertaste. Oatmeal Chocolate Chip Cookies are an obvious fouth because of their delicious flavor and healthy oat touch. Kit Kats have to be fifth because of their amazing wafer and chocolate mix. That leaves Cookies and Cream Ice Cream for sixth place, because it is undoubtedly the best ice cream flavor. Seventh place is take by brownies, the timeless chocolate desert. Berry Pie takes eighth place, as it is an amazing dessert to have by itself or with ice cream. Ninth place has to be Lemon Meringue Pie, even though no one knows how to spell it, it is undoubtedly delicious. And finally, tenth place is take by pumpkin pie, the perfect Thanksgiving dessert.
